# A Love Letter

My dearest,

Words feel inadequate to express the depth of my feelings for you. You are the sunshine in my life, the melody in my heart, and the reason for all my joy.

Here is a small poem for you:

In the quiet space where our spirits meet,
A symphony plays, oh so bittersweet.
Your presence brightens every day,
Chasing all shadows far away.

Forever and always, I love you.
